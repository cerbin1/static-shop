FAQ

What materials are used in your handmade jewelry?

We use a variety of materials including precious metals, gemstones, beads, and other high-quality materials to ensure each piece is unique and durable.

How do I care for my handmade pottery?

Our pottery is made from high-quality clay and glazes. To ensure longevity, hand wash with mild soap and avoid sudden temperature changes to prevent cracking.

Are your candles made from natural ingredients?

Yes, our candles are made from natural soy wax, essential oils, and lead-free cotton wicks to ensure a clean and eco-friendly burn.

Can I request custom handmade items?

Absolutely! We love creating custom pieces. Please contact us with your specific requirements and we will be happy to work with you to create something special.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition. Please refer to our return policy page for more details.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping costs and delivery times will vary based on the destination.

About Crafted Treasures

Crafted Treasures offers a curated collection of handmade jewelry, pottery, candles, knitted goods, paintings, and other crafts. Each piece is crafted with love by talented artisans.
